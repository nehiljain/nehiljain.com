Simon frames link blogging as a third low-stakes/high-value writing
category alongside long-form essays and project descriptions.

<Quote source="Simon Willison" url="https://simonwillison.net/2024/Dec/22/link-blog/">
  Sharing interesting links with commentary is a low effort, high value
  way to contribute to internet life at large.
</Quote>

The guideline that stuck with me: a reader should get value even if
they don't follow the link.
